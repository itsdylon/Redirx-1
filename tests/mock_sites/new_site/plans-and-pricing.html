Plans & Pricing

TechCo offers flexible engagement models designed to match your specific project requirements and budget. Whether you need dedicated consulting support, custom development services, or strategic technology guidance, we have pricing options that work for you.

Every organization has unique needs. Our pricing structures reflect this reality by providing options that align with different project types, durations, and complexity levels.

Engagement Models

Time & Materials

Best suited for projects with evolving requirements or exploratory initiatives where flexibility is paramount. You pay only for the actual hours worked and resources utilized.

Key Benefits:

  • Maximum flexibility to adapt scope as requirements become clearer
  • Pay for actual effort invested, no overpaying for fixed estimates
  • Easy to scale team size up or down as needs change
  • Transparent billing with detailed time tracking and regular reports

Typical Rates:

  • Junior Developer: $95-125/hour
  • Senior Developer: $150-185/hour
  • Solution Architect: $185-225/hour
  • Principal Consultant: $225-275/hour
  • Executive Consultant: $275-325/hour

Rates depend on expertise level, technology stack, and engagement duration. Volume discounts available for long-term commitments.

Fixed Price Projects

Ideal for well-defined projects with clear requirements and deliverables. We provide a comprehensive cost estimate upfront, giving you budget certainty.

Key Benefits:

  • Predictable costs established before work begins
  • Clear deliverables and success criteria defined upfront
  • Reduced management overhead - we handle project execution
  • Risk transfer - we're incentivized to deliver efficiently

Project Sizing:

  • Small Projects (1-3 months): $50K-$150K
  • Medium Projects (3-6 months): $150K-$400K
  • Large Projects (6-12 months): $400K-$1M+

Pricing based on detailed scope analysis, technical complexity, timeline requirements, and team composition. We provide transparent estimates with clear assumptions.

Dedicated Team

Extend your organization with a committed team working exclusively on your initiatives. This model provides continuity, deep product knowledge, and seamless integration with your internal teams.

Key Benefits:

  • Dedicated resources focused entirely on your projects
  • Build institutional knowledge over time
  • Seamless integration with internal teams and processes
  • Flexible team composition adjusted to evolving needs

Monthly Investment:

  • 2-Person Team: $25K-35K/month
  • 5-Person Team: $55K-75K/month
  • 10-Person Team: $100K-140K/month

Teams typically include developers, QA engineers, designers, and project coordinators. Configuration customized to your requirements with monthly billing cycles.

Consulting Retainer

Secure ongoing access to senior expertise for strategic guidance, architecture reviews, technology assessments, and advisory services. Perfect for organizations needing periodic expert input.

Key Benefits:

  • Priority access to senior consultants when needed
  • Proactive strategic guidance to avoid costly mistakes
  • Regular touchpoints to review progress and direction
  • Cost-effective compared to full-time executive hires

Monthly Retainer Options:

  • Basic: 10 hours/month - $4,500
  • Standard: 20 hours/month - $8,500
  • Premium: 40 hours/month - $16,000
  • Enterprise: Custom arrangements available

Unused hours roll over for up to 3 months. Additional hours available at reduced rates for retainer clients.

Pricing Factors

Several factors influence final project costs:

  • Scope & Complexity: Larger, more technically challenging projects require more specialized expertise and time
  • Timeline: Accelerated timelines may require additional resources or premium rates
  • Technology Stack: Emerging or specialized technologies may command higher rates due to skill scarcity
  • Team Seniority: Projects requiring senior architects or specialized experts cost more than those suitable for mid-level developers
  • Location: On-site work in certain locations may include travel and accommodation costs
  • Duration: Longer engagements often qualify for volume discounts
