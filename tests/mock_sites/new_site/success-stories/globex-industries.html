Success Story: Globex Industries Cloud Migration Success

Client: Globex Industries

Industry: Manufacturing

Solution: AWS Cloud Migration

Timeline: 10 months

Team Size: 15 professionals

The Challenge

Globex Industries, a global manufacturing company with operations in 25 countries, faced mounting challenges with their on-premise data center infrastructure. Their aging infrastructure was becoming a barrier to innovation and global expansion.

Critical challenges included:

  • High infrastructure costs with three data centers requiring significant capital investment for maintenance and upgrades
  • Poor reliability with average uptime of only 97%, causing costly production disruptions
  • Long deployment cycles taking weeks to provision new infrastructure, slowing innovation
  • Difficulty scaling to support new markets and seasonal demand fluctuations
  • Limited disaster recovery capabilities putting business continuity at risk
  • Inability to deploy applications in regions close to customers, impacting performance
  • IT team spending 80% of time on infrastructure maintenance rather than innovation

The company needed to modernize its infrastructure to reduce costs, improve reliability, enable faster innovation, and support global expansion plans.

Our Solution

TechCo led a complete cloud migration program, moving over 150 applications and workloads from on-premise data centers to AWS. Our approach balanced speed with risk management, ensuring business continuity throughout the migration.

Migration Strategy

We developed a phased migration approach using the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework:

  • Assessment Phase: Inventoried all applications, dependencies, and infrastructure components
  • Planning Phase: Categorized applications by migration strategy (rehost, replatform, refactor)
  • Wave Planning: Grouped applications into migration waves based on dependencies and complexity
  • Execution: Migrated applications in controlled waves with thorough testing
  • Optimization: Right-sized resources and implemented cost optimization strategies

Technical Implementation

We designed a robust cloud architecture leveraging AWS best practices:

  • Multi-region deployment for disaster recovery and global performance
  • Auto-scaling groups to handle demand fluctuations efficiently
  • Containerized microservices for critical applications using ECS and EKS
  • Serverless functions for event-driven workloads using Lambda
  • Comprehensive monitoring and alerting using CloudWatch and third-party tools
  • Infrastructure as code using Terraform for consistency and repeatability
  • CI/CD pipelines enabling rapid, reliable deployments

Risk Mitigation

We implemented extensive safeguards to ensure smooth migration:

  • Parallel running of on-premise and cloud systems during transition
  • Comprehensive testing including performance, security, and disaster recovery scenarios
  • Detailed runbooks for each migration wave
  • 24/7 support during migration windows
  • Rollback procedures for every application

Results

The cloud migration delivered transformational benefits across infrastructure, operations, and business capabilities:

Cost Savings

  • 40% reduction in infrastructure costs: Eliminated data center leases, reduced hardware spending, and optimized cloud resource usage
  • $3.2M annual savings: Net savings after accounting for cloud costs
  • Converted CapEx to OpEx: Improved cash flow with pay-as-you-go model

Reliability & Performance

  • 99.95% uptime: Up from 97%, reducing costly production disruptions
  • 50% faster application performance: Global content delivery and optimized infrastructure
  • RPO/RTO improvements: Recovery Point Objective reduced to 1 hour, Recovery Time Objective to 4 hours

Operational Efficiency

  • 90% reduction in infrastructure provisioning time: Minutes instead of weeks
  • 50% faster deployment cycles: Automated CI/CD enabling rapid innovation
  • IT team productivity: 80% of time now spent on innovation rather than maintenance

Business Enablement

  • Global expansion enabled: Deployed to 3 new regions in 6 months
  • Seasonal scaling: Easily handle 3x traffic during peak seasons
  • Innovation acceleration: Launched 12 new digital initiatives in first year post-migration

Client Testimonial

Their consultants brought both technical depth and business acumen to the engagement. The migration was completed on time and on budget, with zero business disruption. We now have a modern, scalable infrastructure that enables rather than constrains our business strategy."

Technologies Used

  • Cloud Platform: AWS (EC2, ECS, EKS, Lambda, RDS, S3)
  • Infrastructure as Code: Terraform, CloudFormation
  • Containers: Docker, Kubernetes
  • CI/CD: Jenkins, GitLab CI, AWS CodePipeline
  • Monitoring: CloudWatch, Datadog, New Relic
  • Security: AWS Security Hub, GuardDuty, WAF

Key Success Factors

  • Executive sponsorship and clear business objectives
  • Thorough assessment and planning before execution
  • Phased approach minimizing risk
  • Strong collaboration between TechCo and Globex teams
  • Comprehensive testing and validation
  • Focus on optimization post-migration, not just lift-and-shift
  • Investment in team training and skill development
