Careers at TechCo Solutions

At TechCo Solutions, we're building the future of enterprise technology. We're looking for talented, passionate professionals who want to work on challenging projects, learn cutting-edge technologies, and make a real impact for clients around the world.

Whether you're an experienced engineer, a recent graduate, or someone looking to transition into technology, we offer opportunities to grow your career in a supportive, innovative environment.

Why Work With Us

  • Work on cutting-edge technology projects for leading global companies
  • Collaborative, inclusive culture that values diverse perspectives
  • Competitive compensation and comprehensive benefits package
  • Professional development support including training, certifications, and conference attendance
  • Flexible work arrangements and work-life balance
  • Opportunity to work with and learn from industry experts
  • Clear career progression paths and mentorship programs

Open Positions

Senior Software Engineer

Location: San Francisco, CA or Remote

We're seeking experienced software engineers to join our development teams. You'll work on enterprise applications using modern technologies including cloud platforms, microservices, and containerization.

Requirements: 5+ years of software development experience, strong knowledge of Java or Python, experience with cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP), excellent problem-solving skills.

Solution Architect

Location: New York, NY

Lead the design of comprehensive technology solutions for our enterprise clients. This role requires deep technical expertise combined with excellent communication and client-facing skills.

Requirements: 8+ years of IT experience, proven track record designing enterprise solutions, strong knowledge of architecture patterns, relevant certifications preferred.

UX Designer

Location: San Francisco, CA

Create exceptional user experiences for enterprise applications. You'll conduct user research, design interfaces, and collaborate closely with development teams to bring designs to life.

Requirements: 3+ years of UX design experience, proficiency with Figma and Adobe Creative Suite, strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design.

DevOps Engineer

Location: Remote

Build and maintain our CI/CD pipelines, cloud infrastructure, and deployment automation. Experience with AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and infrastructure-as-code required.

Requirements: 4+ years of DevOps experience, strong scripting skills (Python, Bash), experience with containerization and orchestration, AWS certification a plus.

Benefits & Perks

  • Competitive salary with performance-based bonuses
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company matching
  • Generous paid time off and flexible holidays
  • Remote work options and flexible schedules
  • Annual professional development budget
  • Latest technology and equipment
  • Team building events and social activities

How to Apply

To apply for any of our open positions, please send your resume and a brief cover letter to careers@techcosolutions.com. Include the position title in the subject line.

We review all applications carefully and will contact qualified candidates within two weeks. Please note that due to the volume of applications, we may not be able to respond to every submission individually.

TechCo Solutions is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
