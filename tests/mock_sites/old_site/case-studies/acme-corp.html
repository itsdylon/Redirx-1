Case Study: Acme Corp Custom CRM Transformation

Client: Acme Corp

Industry: Retail

Solution: Custom CRM Development

Timeline: 8 months

Team Size: 12 professionals

The Challenge

Acme Corp, a leading retail organization with over 500 stores across North America, was struggling with outdated legacy systems that hindered their sales team's effectiveness. Their existing CRM was built on 15-year-old technology that couldn't keep pace with modern business requirements.

Key challenges included:

  • Sales representatives spending 3-4 hours daily on manual data entry instead of selling
  • Lack of mobile access forcing reps to return to office to update customer information
  • No integration between CRM and other business systems, creating data silos
  • Poor reporting capabilities making it difficult to track sales performance and forecast accurately
  • Customer complaints about inconsistent service due to incomplete customer history
  • High maintenance costs for the aging system with frequent outages

The company needed a modern solution that would improve sales productivity, enhance customer experiences, and provide better visibility into sales operations.

Our Solution

TechCo Solutions partnered with Acme Corp to design and implement a comprehensive custom CRM solution tailored to their specific business processes and requirements. Our approach combined industry best practices with Acme's unique workflows.

Discovery & Planning

We began with an intensive 6-week discovery phase, interviewing sales representatives, managers, and executives to understand their workflows, pain points, and requirements. We documented over 200 user stories and created detailed process maps showing how sales activities would flow through the new system.

Technical Architecture

We designed a cloud-native architecture built on AWS to ensure scalability, reliability, and security:

  • React-based web application for desktop users
  • Native mobile apps for iOS and Android with offline capabilities
  • RESTful APIs enabling integration with existing systems
  • PostgreSQL database with automated backups and disaster recovery
  • Microservices architecture allowing independent scaling of components

Key Features

  • 360-degree customer view consolidating data from multiple sources
  • Automated data capture reducing manual entry by 80%
  • AI-powered lead scoring and opportunity prioritization
  • Real-time sales dashboards and customizable reports
  • Integration with email, calendar, and marketing automation systems
  • Mobile-first design enabling field sales productivity

Implementation Approach

We used an agile methodology with 2-week sprints, delivering working software incrementally and incorporating feedback continuously. This approach allowed Acme to see progress regularly and make course corrections as needed.

Results

The new CRM system transformed Acme Corp's sales operations, delivering significant improvements across all key metrics:

Quantifiable Outcomes

  • 45% improvement in sales productivity: Sales reps now spend 3+ hours more per day on selling activities rather than administrative tasks
  • 30% increase in customer satisfaction scores: Better access to customer history enables more personalized service
  • 60% reduction in data entry time: Automated capture and intelligent defaults minimize manual work
  • $2.4M annual cost savings: Reduced IT maintenance costs and improved operational efficiency
  • 99.9% system uptime: Up from 95% with the legacy system
  • 23% increase in sales: Improved productivity and customer insights driving revenue growth

Qualitative Benefits

  • Sales team satisfaction improved dramatically with modern, intuitive tools
  • Management gained real-time visibility into sales pipeline and performance
  • Customer service improved through access to complete customer history
  • Reduced training time for new sales representatives
  • Foundation for future innovation with extensible architecture

Client Testimonial

This project exceeded our expectations in every way."

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: React, TypeScript, Material-UI
  • Backend: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
  • Mobile: React Native for iOS and Android
  • Cloud: AWS (EC2, RDS, S3, CloudFront)
  • Integration: REST APIs, GraphQL
  • DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins CI/CD
