Pricing & Engagement Models

TechCo Solutions offers flexible engagement models designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you're a startup looking for development support or an enterprise seeking comprehensive consulting services, we have pricing options that align with your requirements and budget.

Our transparent pricing approach ensures you understand exactly what you're paying for and the value you're receiving. We work with you to structure engagements that maximize your return on investment.

Engagement Models

Time & Materials

Pay for actual hours worked with complete flexibility to adjust scope and priorities. Ideal for projects with evolving requirements or ongoing support engagements.

  • Hourly rates based on role and experience level
  • Monthly billing with detailed time tracking
  • Flexibility to scale team up or down
  • No long-term commitment required
  • Full transparency into time allocation

Fixed Price Projects

Agreed-upon price for clearly defined deliverables and timeline. Best for projects with well-defined scope and requirements.

  • Detailed scope of work and acceptance criteria
  • Milestone-based payments
  • Predictable budgeting
  • Risk sharing between client and TechCo Solutions
  • Change request process for scope modifications

Dedicated Team

A dedicated team works exclusively on your projects with long-term commitment. Perfect for organizations needing ongoing development capacity.

  • Team dedicated to your projects
  • Monthly retainer pricing
  • Deep integration with your organization
  • 3-12 month minimum commitment
  • Consistent team members for knowledge continuity

Consulting Retainer

Reserve a set number of consulting hours each month for strategic guidance and advisory services.

  • Guaranteed availability from senior consultants
  • Priority scheduling for urgent needs
  • Monthly or quarterly retainer options
  • Unused hours may roll over (terms apply)
